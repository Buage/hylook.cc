hylook.cc - Privacy Policy

What information do we collect

When using hylook.cc, we only collect what's required for the service to work. This includes:

  • The BCrypt hashed username you searched for
  • A random token generated to create an AI rarity analysis
  • The date and time when you searched for the username

How we use your information

We only use the collected data to provide hylook.cc services, count views, and prevent any abuses or malicious activity.

Data retention

We save usernames and related information until we choose to delete them for any abuse, security reason, or maintenance. The generated token will never be associated with your real identity. It's deleted after generating an AI rarity analysis.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not share any of your data with third parties, unless required by law or needed to prevent illegal or abusive activity. We never sell your data for advertising purposes.

Security

We implement measures to protect your data and links from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no system can be 100% secure.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may change or update this privacy policy at any time. If you continue using hylook.cc, it means you accept the updated policy.
